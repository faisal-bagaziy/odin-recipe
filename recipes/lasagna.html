Lasagna

description of the Recipe

lasgana is italian dish which is made from meat, pasta, tomato sauce and cheese!

you will need:

  • 200gram meat
  • 150gram pasta
  • 250liter tomato sauce
  • 1kilo cheese!!!

steps:

  1. add the meat to a pan
  2. after cooking the meat add the pasta with all the sauces
  3. put it in the oven for 45min
  4. let it rest for 15min
